Welcome to our Wedding

We are so excited to celebrate our marriage with everyone we love on August 17, 2024 in the beautiful state of Vermont! We hope our wedding will not only emboy our love story, but also our love for the area and community, as we will be sourcing everything locally from catering to florals to refreshments. Catering will be provided by the delicious Flatbread Pizza Company, and a wide selection of local craft beer and wine will be present. Attire will be cocktail or semi-formal, and we highly recommend the ladies to either wear flats or chunky heels to avoid sinking in the grass. There will be an assortment of lawn games, and our dog, Chance, will be joining us for the festivities.

About Burlington, VT & D.A.R. State Park

Bulington has held a special place in our relationship. It's where we go to get away from the hustle and bustle of the city, eat great food, drink good beer, breathe fresh air, spend time with family, and explore all the stunning nature Vermont has to offer. The incredible hiking of the Long Trail, the killer skiing at Sugarbush and Stowe, and the serene waters of Lake Champlain keep us coming back every year.

While the above locations are household names, there are some hidden gems in Vermont. We stumbled upon DAR on the way home Memorial Day Weekend this year and instantly fell in love with the views and the peacefulness of the park. We hope you will enjoy it as much as we do!
