Camping: There are 47 tent/RV sites and 24 lean-to sites available to rent.
Fishing: Lots of fishing options in Lake Champlain.
Picnicking: D.A.R. is a great place for a picnic. There is also a picnic pavilion that is available to rent.
Horseshoes: There are two horseshoe pits at the park.
Volleyball: There is a volleyball net in the day use field.

Welcome to our Wedding

We are so excited to welcome you to our wedding on August 17, 2024 and can't wait to celebrate with everyone. Food will be provided by the incredible Flatbread Pizza Company and a wide selection of local beer and wine will be present. Attire will be on the more casual side, as the venue is partially outside and has grassy areas (stiletto style heels would not be our recommendation). There will be lawn games and our dog Change will be joining us for the festivities. We hope you can make it!

About Burlington, VT & D.A.R. State Park

Bulington has been a special part of our relationship. Its where we go to get away, eat great food, breathe the fresh air, see family, and our homebase for exploring all the stunning nature Vermont has to offer. The incredible hiking of the long trail, the killer skiing at Sugarbush and Stowe, and the serene waters of lake champlain.

While some of the names above are household staples, Vermont also hides some hidden gems.We found DAR on an earlier trip to Vermont and instantly fell in love with the views and the peacefulness of the park. We hope you will enjoy it as much as we do!
